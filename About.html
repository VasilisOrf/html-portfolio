About Me

As a Senior HR Consultant and Career Coach, I bring over five years of expertise in Human Resources, Talent Acquisition, Learning & Development, and HR Operations. My passion lies in empowering professionals to unlock their full potential, whether by navigating complex career transitions, sharpening their skills, or thriving in today’s dynamic job market. With a proven track record of aligning individual aspirations with organizational objectives, I bridge the gap between personal growth and business success.


Who I Am

With a Master’s in Business Administration (MBA) specializing in Human Resources and a BSc in Information & Communications Technology, I bring a unique blend of technical and people-focused expertise. Throughout my career, I’ve worked with diverse teams across Europe, successfully guiding over 300 professionals into new roles, coaching them through career changes, and optimizing their professional branding.


What I Do

I provide tailored support to help you stand out in the competitive job market. From resume building and LinkedIn optimization to mock interviews and networking strategies, I help clients define and achieve their career goals. I also design and deliver L&D programs to upskill teams and individuals, leveraging my deep understanding of HR processes, employee experience, and change management.
